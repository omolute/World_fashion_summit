WFS2025

Our aim is to achieve a carbon-neutral fashion sector on equality, social justice, animal welfare, and ecological integrity and balance our economic, environmental and social needs, allowing prosperity for now and future generations by providing a premier interdisciplinary platform for researchers, practitioners and educators to present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, and concerns as well as practical challenges encountered and solutions adopted in the Fashion Industry.

Our Mission

To achieve a carbon-neutral fashion sector on equality, social justice, animal welfare and ecological integrity.

Why Attend?

By attending the World Fashion Summit, you’ll become part of a vibrant community driving positive change in the fashion industry. Don’t miss this opportunity to learn, connect, and contribute to a more ethical and sustainable future!

LEARN MORE

Our Vision

Uniting the global fashion industry to transform the way fashion is designed, produced, consumed and discarded, to create a sustainable and ethical fashion ecosystem.
